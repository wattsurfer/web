We offers Load Forecasting, Asset optimization & portfolio optimization services to energy utilities to minimize electricity procurement cost.

Forecasting

We offer best day-ahead and mid-term load demand forecasting in the market through the power of artificial intellegence. This minimizes unwanted balancing penalties, over-under procurement (or sale) of electricity, and optimizes regulating and emergency capacities.

Asset Optimisation

Through our market knowledge we provide strategies to the small and large power generators to optimize their assets and become future proof in the process of fast energy transition. We also provide insights into providing strategic balacing dispatch services to increase the revenue while supporting the grid stability.

Portfolio Optimisation

We offer strategic portfolio insights to optimize trading through the EPEX spot exchange, direct forward and futures contracts to minimize the electricity procurement cost. We also aim to maximize utilization of non-expensive and clean green electricity.
